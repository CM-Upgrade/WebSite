Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UpgradeMate collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit upgrademate.io, request a free trial, contact us, or purchase a license.

1. Collection and Use of Your Information

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect the information you submit through our forms:

  • Contact and trial requests: name, email, company name, and information you include in your message or trial request (such as SCCM environment details).
  • Purchases: billing name, email, billing address, and tax identification number where required for invoicing.

1.2 Payment Information

Card payments are processed by a licensed payment service provider. Your full card details (card number, expiry, CVV) are submitted directly to the payment provider's secure environment. We never see or store your full card details. We only receive a transaction reference, masked card information, and the result of the payment.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps. We use this information to operate and secure the site.

1.4 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Process your trial, purchase, invoice, and license delivery
  • Send transactional emails related to your request or order
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the business (such as payment, hosting, and email infrastructure), and only as necessary for them to perform their service.

2. Storage and Security

2.1 Security

We use TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, apply access controls on our backend systems, and rely on PCI-DSS compliant providers for payment processing. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using commercially reasonable safeguards.

2.2 Retention

We keep your personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law. Purchase records and invoices are retained for 10 years as required by Turkish tax legislation. Other data is deleted once it is no longer needed.

3. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to request data portability. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@upgrademate.io. We will respond within 30 days.

4. Miscellaneous

4.1 Cookies

We use only technical cookies that are strictly necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

4.2 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above indicates when the most recent changes were made.

4.3 Contact

For questions about this Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: info@upgrademate.io
