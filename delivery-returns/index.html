Delivery & Returns

How we deliver your license, and our policy on returns and refunds.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

UpgradeMate is a digital software product. This page explains how your license is delivered after purchase and the terms that apply to returns and refunds.

1. Product and Delivery

1.1 What You Receive

UpgradeMate is delivered digitally. After your payment is successfully processed, you will receive an email containing your license key, the software download link, and installation instructions. No physical goods are shipped.

1.2 Delivery Time

Delivery is made by email within 24 hours after your payment is confirmed. In most cases delivery is much faster. If you have not received your license within 24 hours, please check your spam folder first, then contact us at info@upgrademate.io.

1.3 Delivery Address

Your license is delivered to the email address you provide at checkout. Please make sure this address is correct and that you can receive messages from our domain.

2. Returns and Refunds

2.1 No Refunds After Delivery

UpgradeMate is a digital software product with a license that is delivered electronically and takes effect immediately. Once the license key and download link have been sent to you, the sale is considered complete and refunds are not accepted. By completing your purchase you acknowledge that delivery begins immediately and waive any applicable right of withdrawal for digital goods.

2.2 Try Before You Buy

To help you evaluate whether UpgradeMate fits your environment before you commit, we offer a free 10-day trial. You can request it at upgrademate.io/trynow. We strongly recommend completing the trial before purchasing a license.

2.3 Technical Issues

If you experience a technical problem with your license or with installing or running the software, our support team will help you resolve it. Please contact us at info@upgrademate.io with a description of the issue and we will respond as quickly as possible.

3. Payment and Cancellations

If your payment fails or is not completed, no license is delivered and no charge is retained. If you notice an unauthorized transaction, please contact us immediately at info@upgrademate.io so we can investigate.
